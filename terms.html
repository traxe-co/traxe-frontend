01 About TRAXE

TRAXE (operated as TRAXE.co) is a content verification and registration platform that provides cryptographic timestamping, hash fingerprinting, and origin declaration services for digital content. TRAXE is designed to assist creators, studios, and organisations in documenting the declared origin and submission date of digital assets.

TRAXE operates in support of transparency obligations under the EU AI Act (2024/1689) and related regulatory frameworks. However, TRAXE itself is not a legal authority, certification body, notary, or regulatory agency.

02 Scope of Service — What TRAXE Does and Does Not Do

TRAXE provides the following services:

  • Cryptographic SHA-256 fingerprinting of uploaded files
  • Timestamping of submission date and time
  • Recording of user-declared origin information (e.g. AI-generated, human-made, hybrid)
  • Issuance of a unique TRAXE Verification ID (TRXE-XXXXXXXX)
  • A publicly accessible verification page linked to each submission

TRAXE does not:

  • Verify the accuracy or truthfulness of any information submitted by users

  • Guarantee that submitted content is original, non-infringing, or lawfully owned

A TRAXE certificate records what a user declared at the time of submission. It does not independently verify or validate those declarations.
