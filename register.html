FAQ

Common questions.

Everything you need to know about TRAXE and content verification.

What is a TRAXE ID? ↓
A TRAXE ID is a permanent, publicly verifiable identifier tied to a cryptographic fingerprint (SHA-256 hash) of your content. It proves that a specific file existed at a specific moment in time, and that you declared its origin honestly. Anyone can verify a TRAXE ID at any time.
Can a TRAXE certificate be faked or altered? ↓
No. Each certificate is cryptographically signed using Ed25519 — a state-of-the-art digital signature algorithm. The certificate is tied to the exact file content: if the file changes even by a single pixel, the hash no longer matches. Records are immutable and cannot be altered after creation.
Does TRAXE detect whether content is AI-generated? ↓
No — TRAXE is a declaration platform, not a detection tool. The creator declares the origin of their content. This transparency-first model aligns with the EU AI Act's requirements for disclosed AI content. TRAXE verifies that the declaration was made, not that it's accurate.
Who is TRAXE designed for? ↓
TRAXE is built for photographers, videographers, creative studios, advertising agencies, and any professional who produces visual content — whether AI-generated or human-made. It's particularly valuable for teams working with AI tools who need to demonstrate compliance with emerging regulations.
What is the EU AI Act, and does TRAXE help with compliance? ↓
The EU AI Act (coming into force 2026) requires disclosure of AI-generated content in certain contexts, especially in advertising, media, and public communications. TRAXE provides a standardised, verifiable way to disclose AI involvement — supporting compliance without replacing legal advice.
Is the first verification really free? ↓
Yes. Every new account includes 5 free verifications per month on the Starter plan, permanently. No credit card required. Paid plans unlock more verifications, API access, and custom branding.
