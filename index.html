FAQ

Common questions.

Everything you need to know about TRAXE.co and content verification.

What is a TRAXE ID? ↓
A TRAXE ID is a permanent, publicly verifiable identifier tied to a cryptographic fingerprint (SHA-256 hash) of your content. It proves that a specific file existed at a specific moment in time, and that you declared its origin honestly. Anyone can verify a TRAXE ID at any time.
Can a TRAXE certificate be faked or altered? ↓
No. Each certificate is cryptographically signed using Ed25519 — a state-of-the-art digital signature algorithm. The certificate is tied to the exact file content: if the file changes even by a single pixel, the hash no longer matches. Records are immutable and cannot be altered after creation.
Does TRAXE.co detect whether content is AI-generated? ↓
Yes — TRAXE.co runs an automated AI detection scan on every upload using SightEngine's machine learning models, providing a confidence score. The creator also declares the origin of their content. This dual-layer approach aligns with the EU AI Act's requirements for transparent AI content disclosure.
Who is TRAXE.co designed for? ↓
TRAXE.co is built for photographers, videographers, creative studios, advertising agencies, and any professional who produces visual content — whether AI-generated or human-made. It's particularly valuable for teams working with AI tools who need to demonstrate compliance with emerging regulations.
What is the EU AI Act, and does TRAXE.co help with compliance? ↓
The EU AI Act (coming into force 2026) requires disclosure of AI-generated content in certain contexts, especially in advertising, media, and public communications. TRAXE.co provides a standardised, verifiable way to disclose AI involvement — supporting compliance without replacing legal advice.
Is the first verification really free? ↓
Yes. Every new account includes 3 free verifications on the Starter plan. No credit card required. Paid plans unlock more verifications, API access, and custom branding.

Our Story
Why TRAXE.co exists.
"The generation after us will inherit a world where nothing looks fake — because everything can be. I built TRAXE.co so that honesty has infrastructure."

TRAXE.co was born from a simple observation: the creative industry was moving faster than its integrity could keep up with. AI image generation went from novelty to industry standard in under two years. Campaigns, editorials, product shots, news imagery — all of it became indistinguishable from reality. And nobody was keeping score.

As a creative director and founder of Vizionaire, an AI visual studio building cinematic brand campaigns, Asha Gwendolyn Bastian sat at the intersection of this shift daily. She saw brands embrace AI without disclosure. She saw photographers lose commissions to tools that mimicked their style. She watched the EU AI Act take shape — not as a threat, but as an invitation.

The .co in TRAXE.co is intentional. This isn't a corporate filing. It's a commitment. A content certificate isn't just compliance paperwork — it's a creative's handshake with their audience. TRAXE.co exists to make that handshake verifiable, permanent, and effortless. For the generation building with AI, and the generation that will judge how we did it.
