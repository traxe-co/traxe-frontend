FAQ

Common questions.

Is TRAXE legally binding?

TRAXE creates a declaration-based, independently verifiable record of what you claim about your content. This is aligned with the EU AI Act's transparency requirements for AI-generated material. While TRAXE is not a court document, it provides an auditable, timestamped disclosure record that can be presented to regulators, platforms, and clients as evidence of compliance intent.

Does TRAXE detect if content is AI-generated?

No — and intentionally so. No detection tool available today is reliable enough to make a legal or commercial verdict. TRAXE is declaration-based: you declare the origin of your content and it is independently certified. This is the correct model for EU AI Act compliance, which requires disclosure, not detection.

What happens if I run out of verifications?

You'll be prompted to upgrade before submitting additional content. Your existing verifications remain fully accessible and valid — nothing expires. Upgrade at any time and continue from where you left off.

Can I use TRAXE for client campaigns?

Yes — this is the primary use case. Upload the campaign asset, declare origin and tools, and share the verify link with your client. They can independently verify the record at any time with no TRAXE account required. Studio and Agency plans include downloadable badge assets for embedding directly in campaign deliverables.
